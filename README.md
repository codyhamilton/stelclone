# Free Frontier

A 2D real-time strategy game set in the depths of space, where corporate greed meets alien diplomacy in an unregulated frontier.

## Game Overview

Free Frontier is a strategic space management game that puts you in control of a corporate entity vying for dominance in an unregulated sector of space. As the CEO of a space corporation, you'll navigate the complex dynamics of resource exploitation, alien diplomacy, and worker management in your quest for market supremacy.

### Core Gameplay Elements

- **Sector Control**: Manage and expand your influence across a dynamic space sector
- **Resource Management**: Mine valuable resources from asteroids and manage your supply chain
- **Fleet Management**: Control and expand your fleet of mining vessels and military ships
- **Colonization**: Establish mining colonies on asteroids to extract resources
- **Diplomacy**: Interact with alien factions, each with unique characteristics and agendas
- **Corporate Management**: Balance worker satisfaction with profit margins
- **Market Dynamics**: Compete for market dominance and influence your corporation's stock price

## Game Theme

Free Frontier explores themes of corporate exploitation and unregulated capitalism in a space setting. Players must balance:
- Profit maximization vs. worker stability
- Limited resource exploitation in a competitive environment
- Diplomatic relations vs. Profit maximisation
- Short-term gains vs. long-term sustainability

## Technical Details

- Built with C/SDL3
- 2D graphics with modern visual effects
- Real-time simulation of space sector dynamics
- Complex AI for alien faction behavior
- Robust economy and market simulation
- Advanced ship customization system
- Dynamic resource management
- Real-time combat simulation
